Adventurers go on Quests, slay bosses in Dungeons & Raids, and train up in the training ground to progress through Nightvale.ClassesThe Adventurer is divided into 3 classes: the Warrior, the Mage, & the Marksman.
Each class specializes in different weaponry and armor types, but all share the same goal: to fill their pouches with $GOLD.In end-game content, the attack style of each class will affect your efficiency against certain bosses. This creates the need for correct composition in certain features.
There are 3584 Warriors, 3583 Marksmen, & 3583 Mages.<div class="grid-area-1-1 z-[1] justify-self-start text-left leading-snug mt-[0.5em]">Combat LevelAn Adventurer's combat level determines the rewards they receive in Quests and their chances of completing Dungeons and Raids.
